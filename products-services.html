Products

Check Our Products

Clinosys Hospital Information System (CHIS)

This is our all-in-one core system that connects every department in your hospital. It brings all of our powerful modules together for seamless management and operations.

  • Fully Integrated Platform
  • Centralized Data Management
  • Scalable for Future Growth

Patient Management System(PMS)

Effortlessly manage the entire patient journey from their first registration to their final discharge. This module handles all OPD, IPD, admissions, and transfers in one place.

  • Streamlined Patient Registration
  • Complete OPD/IPD Workflow
  • Easy Admission & Discharge Tracking

Billing & Financial Management

Simplify your hospital's finances with our comprehensive billing module. It easily handles insurance claims, corporate billing, and TPA management with real-time reporting.

  • Automated Billing & Invoicing
  • Insurance & TPA Claim Management
  • Real-time Financial Analytics

Electronic Medical Records (EMR)

Go paperless with our secure and user-friendly EMR system. Doctors can instantly access complete patient histories, create digital prescriptions, and view lab reports.

  • Secure Digital Patient Charts
  • Instant Access to Medical History
  • Integrated Lab & Prescription Data

Pharmacy & Inventory Management

Take full control of your pharmacy with real-time stock tracking and automated alerts. Manage suppliers and inventory efficiently to prevent shortages and reduce waste.

  • Real-time Stock & Expiry Tracking
  • Automated Reorder Management
  • Integrated Supplier Database

Laboratory & Radiology Management

Automate your diagnostic workflows from test orders to final reports. Our system manages both laboratory (LIS) and radiology (RIS) information seamlessly.

  • Automated Test & Sample Tracking
  • Seamless Lab Machine Integration
  • Instant Report Generation & Delivery

Operation Theater Management

Efficiently schedule and manage all your operation theaters with ease. This module helps you track surgery details, manage anesthesia records, and optimize OT utilization.

  • Drag-and-Drop Surgery Scheduling
  • Comprehensive Anesthesia Records
  • OT Resource & Staff Management

Doctor & Staff Management

Organize your most valuable asset—your people. Our system helps you create duty rosters, manage schedules, and integrate with payroll for hassle-free staff management.

  • Easy Duty Roster Creation
  • Integrated Payroll & HR Functions
  • Performance & Task Tracking

Mobile Apps for Patients & Doctors

Engage with your patients and empower your doctors with our dedicated mobile apps. Provide convenient, on-the-go access to appointments, health records, and reports.

  • Online Appointment Booking
  • Secure Access to Medical Reports
  • Doctor & Patient Communication Tools
